Proxmark Scripts

This is just a small collection of scripts that are useful when playing with the Proxmark.

- pm3_bin2eml.py: Convert binary dumps to emulator files.
- pm3_eml2bin.py: Convert emulator files to binary dumps.
- lrc_checksum.py: Calculate UID LRC checksums.
